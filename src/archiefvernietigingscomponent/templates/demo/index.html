Record management is the practice of destroying data-records that have met their end-of-life time. "Zaakgericht werken" prescribes the archiving terms for cases that have been brought to an end.

The Archiefvernietigingscomponent (AVC) provides functionality for record managers to schedule destruction of cases according to the archiving parameters. It implements a multi-step, multi-role accordance process.

{% if settings.AVC_DEMO_MODE %}

The demo mode is enabled and allows you to use this application, as seen from different roles. When you open the application, a tutorial is shown on the right side of the screen that explains all steps. You can freely explore this demo and even see the results in the configured Open Zaak instance. The demo data will be reset multiple times per day.
